About IERWF

Indian Educational Research & Welfare Foundation

Welcome to the Indian Educational Research & Welfare Foundation (IERWF), a platform dedicated to fostering meaningful connections between students and employers, providing authentic insights into Indian universities, and extending financial assistance and career opportunities to empower students. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic and the professional world, ensuring that students receive the support they need to pursue their academic and career aspirations.

IERWF is hosting a program in sponsorship with Career Width and Welcoi where students will be given a chance to share their university experiences, insights into their academic journey, and personal development stories through engaging videos. Our unique program aims to empower university students in the fields of Art and Design, Business Studies, Liberal Arts, Engineering Universities and many more areas through financial assistance, career insights, and invaluable experiences. By participating, students stand a chance to win lucrative scholarships and guaranteed internships, paving the way for their professional success.

Our Values

Connectivity

We believe in fostering meaningful connections between students and employers to create a platform for exchanging valuable information about various industries and career opportunities.

Authenticity

Providing real insights into Indian universities, ensuring students make well-informed decisions about their academic journey.

Empowerment

Extending financial assistance and creating avenues for career development within key industries to empower students in pursuing their academic and career aspirations.

Awareness

Generating awareness about diverse career prospects and facilitating placements to enhance students' professional growth.

Recognition

Recognizing and rewarding student achievements, emphasising the pivotal role played by universities in fostering a conducive learning environment.
