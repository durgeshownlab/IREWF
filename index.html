Our Mission

Welcome to the Indian Educational Research & Welfare Foundation (IERWF), a platform dedicated to fostering meaningful connections between students and employers, providing authentic insights into Indian universities, and extending financial assistance and career opportunities to empower students. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic and the professional world, ensuring that students receive the support they need to pursue their academic and career aspirations.

Our mission is to empower students with the resources and opportunities needed for holistic personal and professional growth. We aim to be a catalyst for positive change, fostering a community of well-rounded individuals who contribute meaningfully to society.

Scholarship Program Overview

The IERWF Scholarship Program is a nationwide initiative that is primarily aimed at recognizing and supporting outstanding university students across four key industry areas.

Art & Design

Business Management

Liberal Arts

Special Schools

This program offers a unique opportunity for students to showcase their academic journey, college support, and personal development through video submissions. Winners of this program will be awarded a ₹1 Lakh scholarship for each industry. They will also be receiving a two-month guaranteed internship along with a scholarship to enhance their practical experiences.

Who Can Participate?

The program is open for all the university students who are passionate about sharing their educational journey, campus experiences, and aspirations for the future. Whether they are an aspiring artist, a budding entrepreneur, a liberal thinker, or dedicated to special education, this program welcomes everyone’s unique perspective.

Scholarship Details

Four scholarships, each worth ₹1 Lakh, will be awarded to outstanding students representing the four key industries.

Additionally, winners will receive a two-month guaranteed internship opportunity to further enhance their skills and industry exposure.

How Students Can Benefit?

Participating in the IERWF program offers students a multitude of benefits:

  • Opportunity to showcase their university experiences and achievements.
  • Chance to win prestigious scholarships and secure valuable internships.
  • Platform to connect with industry professionals and explore career opportunities.
  • Recognition for academic excellence and personal development.
  • Empowerment to make informed educational and career decisions.
