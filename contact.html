Your To-Do List in the Program

Attend Webinar 1: Learn about the scholarship contest's modality and framework, featuring representatives from all participating universities.

Participate in Webinar 2: Join fellow students for a detailed briefing on the scholarship contest and video shoot details.

Submit Feedback: Fill out the polling feedback form provided during the webinar and ensure to meet the deadline for submitting your video.

Prepare Your Video: Craft your video submission according to the contest guidelines and ensure it aligns with the theme and requirements.

Advance to 1st Round: Universities will shortlist the top 3 videos from their respective student submissions.

Advance to 2nd Round: Organizers will shortlist the top 20 videos in each industry category based on creativity, relevance, and impact.

YouTube Feature: If selected, your video will be posted on the program's YouTube channel for public viewing.

Online Voting: Engage in online voting for the best video during the 3rd Round. Encourage your friends, family, and network to support your submission.

Industry Review: Industry panelists will review the top 4 videos with the highest votes to determine the ultimate winners.

Internship Opportunity: Scholarship winners will be guaranteed a two-month internship opportunity to further enhance their skills and experience.

Submission

Eligibility Criteria

Open to all university students within the specified industries: Art & Design, Business Management, Liberal Arts, and Special Child Universities.

Students must submit a video (5-10 minutes) covering various aspects outlined in the submission criteria.
